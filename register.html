QUIZZITICH

From Mr. Darcy to Begum Jaan, we have it all here. Showcase your knowledge by taking a deep dive into the world of literature. Welcome to "Quizzitch", the Literature Quiz organised as a part of the Presidency University Literature Festival 1.0, by the century-old Presidency College Society for Acquisition of General Knowledge (PCSAGK).

Rules and Regulations:
  • 1. The quiz is restricted to college/university/institute students only.
  • 2 There will be a total of two rounds for this event.
  • a. Prelims Round which will be conducted in written format. The best 6 teams shall advance to the finals. In case of a tiebreaker, starred questions will be asked.
  • b. Finals - The six teams shall compete against each other by answering a total of 30 questions. There will be a provision for infinite pounce and bounce in this round.
  • 3. Each team shall consist of a maximum of two members. Cross-college teams are allowed as well.
  • 4. Reporting Time - 1.30 p.m.

KAVYAAVRITTI

Poem Recitation

Writing poems has been a cathartic experience altogether, But this whole experience will be elevated when you can share this experience- live with an eager audience. Let the poet living in the pages of old diaries materialise through the event of poem recitation- ‘KAVYAAVRITTI”, an initiative by Presidency University Literature Festival 1.0.

Rules and Regulations:
  • 1. Recitation of poems written only in English, Hindi, or Bangla.
  • 2. Participants will not be allowed to read out.
  • 3. Time duration - 3 minutes
  • 4. Reporting Time - 3.30 p.m.
